City Scale intervention

The demand for child care is steadily increasing, with more and more suburbian parents leaving home to go off to work near to the city


The CBD can seem like a place that is hectic and busy.

Site

Central Station

It is predominantly on the same site as the central station, in the Sydney CBD area.

Close to city centers and main public transport.

The site is within the vicinity of highly dense workplaces per city block.

The Solution Proposal


  • Create amusement and public spaces to encourage social interaction and also engage with the child care sector.
  • A key transition place for parents to meet with children and carers during office breaks.
  • Encouraging more child care facilities and private carers to make use of the facilities available at the amusement park.
  • Having an multi-functional amusement park near the CBD area will allow the city to be more livable.
  • The design aims to strenghten the city's social aspects by orienting toward the 'Family Culture'.
